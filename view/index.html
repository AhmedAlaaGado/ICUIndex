About

A huge number of life threatening conditions usually lose their lives during the search for empty places in the intensive care units. Therefore, knowing the available places in the intensive care unit in different hospitals will decrease such number of deaths and will decrease the estimated time to reach such unit.

FAQ

-what is 'ICUIndex.com' ?

we are a non-profitable organization aims to help saving as many lives as possible by helping patients to reach their desired Intensive Care Units ASAP.

-why should I use 'ICUIndex.com'?

Because 'ICUIndex' is the easiest, fastest and least expensive way to reach your desired ICU type and save your beloved ones.

-Can I search 'ICUIndex.com'?

we provide a consecutive navigation list for easier and faster way to browse through our website.

-Can I login to the website?

No, using our website requires no access to the ordinary user. The login is limited only to the system admins and hospital specialists.

-How can I contact your organization?

You can easily contact us via the 'Contact us' tab at the left bottom of our website page. We will carefully recive your emails and reply ASAP. *Abusive emails are excluded though.

-Can I make a reservation via the website?

Unfortunately no, but we provide many details about the chosen hospital. So you can use the provided phone number to check if phone resirvation is possible.

-How can I access your system?

You can access our system via our website here or via our android app on your smartphone. *you can find it here.

-How do your organization collect this data?

Our system carfully collects and updates this data with association with ministry of health to provide the most accurate information to our users.
